Zorin Facer has emerged as one of the most influential and dynamic figures in the mountain biking world, captivating audiences worldwide with his fearless riding, creative content, and authentic personality. Starting out with nothing more than a secondhand GoPro and a passion for downhill trails, Zorin began documenting his rides in the backwoods of British Columbia, blending cinematic visuals with raw, adrenaline-fueled stunts that quickly drew the attention of MTB enthusiasts across social media. What sets Zorin apart is not just his technical skill or daring descents, but his storytelling ability—each video feels like a mini adventure, complete with crashes, triumphs, and moments of unexpected introspection. As his channel exploded past 2 million subscribers, major bike brands, gear manufacturers, and even travel companies began partnering with him, recognizing his power to influence the next generation of riders. Despite his rapid rise, Zorin remains deeply connected to his roots, frequently featuring lesser-known trails, spotlighting local riders, and encouraging sustainable trail building and maintenance. His signature series, “Facer’s Flow,” has become a staple in the MTB community, combining high-energy trail edits with behind-the-scenes looks at training, gear testing, and the mental challenges of extreme sports. With a reputation for pushing boundaries both creatively and physically, Zorin Facer is not just riding trails—he’s blazing them, leaving a lasting impact on both digital media and mountain biking culture.
